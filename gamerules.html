Rules

⇦

Turn?

Players take it in turns to roll the dice, then select a column to drop their dice (by clicking on the arrow).

Score?

The total eyes of all the dice on each players' game board is their total score. However, if a player can put 2 of the same dice in one column the dice-score for that column is doubled, and for 3 of the same dice, the score is trippled

Game End?

The game ends when any player manages to fill their game-board with dice.The player with the highest totalscore (visible on the left hand side of the screen) is the winner for that game. Total game-victories are tracked for each player during that gaming session.

Replay/Reset?

Players can stop their currently active game by clicking on "Play"/"Replay". If players want to reset the total game-victories for the current session, that's possible with the "Reset" button.
